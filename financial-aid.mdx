<div className="faq-section">
  <AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="How can I pay for college if I don't qualify for financial aid?">
      Several options are available:
      - Payment plans
      - Scholarships
      - Work-study
      - Foundation grants
      
      Learn more at [Dallas College Payment Options](https://www.dallascollege.edu/paying-for-college/pages/default.aspx)
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="What scholarships are available?">
      There are a variety of scholarships available through Dallas College. Visit our [Scholarships Page](https://www.dallascollege.edu/paying-for-college/scholarships-aid/pages/default.aspx) to explore all available options.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="I've exhausted my financial aid. What can I do now?">
      If you've exhausted your financial aid, you can:
      1. Schedule an appointment with the Financial Aid office
      2. Check for additional scholarships and grants
      3. Look into private loans
      4. Explore work-study or part-time jobs
      
      Contact Financial Aid at 972-669-6400 or FinancialAid@DallasCollege.edu
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="I need to drop a class. Will I lose my scholarship?">
      As long as you are enrolled in the appropriate amount of hours, you should remain eligible for your scholarship. However, we recommend scheduling a meeting with your academic advisor to ensure alignment with your graduation plans.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Where is my refund?">
      To check your refund status:
      1. Log into your student portal to access your account
      2. Contact Financial Aid at 972-669-6400 or FinancialAid@DallasCollege.edu
    </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>
</div>
