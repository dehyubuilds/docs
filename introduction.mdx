## What is Career Path Navigator?

Career Path Navigator is an intelligent platform that helps students discover the best courses to take based on their career goals, powered by your university's curriculum data. Our AI-powered assistant acts as a personalized guide, helping students navigate academic decisions aligned with specific career paths.
