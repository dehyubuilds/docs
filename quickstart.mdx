## Getting Started

Learn how to use Career Path Assistant to plan your academic journey and achieve your career goals.

### Set up your profile

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user" title="Create your account">
    To get started with Career Path Assistant:
    1. Visit our [signup page](https://app.careerpathassistant.com/signup)
    2. Create an account using your university email
    3. Complete your profile with your academic interests and career goals
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="university" title="Connect your university">
    Connect your university's curriculum data:
    1. Select your university from our supported institutions
    2. Grant access to your academic records (optional)
    3. Choose your current program and year of study
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Start planning your path

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="route" title="Explore career paths">
    Discover potential career paths:
    1. Browse through different career options
    2. View required skills and courses
    3. Compare different paths and their requirements
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="list-check" title="Get course recommendations">
    Receive personalized course suggestions:
    1. Select your target career path
    2. View recommended courses for your program
    3. Create your academic plan
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
