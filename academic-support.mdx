<div className="support-section">
  <AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="I'm struggling in my classes. Where can I get help?">
      We offer multiple support services:
      1. Free tutoring services at the learning commons located at each campus
      2. Academic coaching
      3. Time management workshops
      4. Faculty office hours
      
      Visit the [Learning Commons](https://www.dallascollege.edu/resources/learning-commons/pages/default.aspx) for more information.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="What do I do if I miss a deadline or class?">
      If you miss a deadline or class:
      1. Contact your instructor immediately
      2. Review the syllabus for instructions
      3. Document your circumstances
      4. Request make-up options
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="How do I access my online class?">
      To access your online class:
      1. Check your email for instructions from your instructor
      2. Log into the student portal
      3. Contact technical support if needed
      4. Email your instructor for assistance
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="What mental health resources are available?">
      We offer comprehensive support including:
      - Free counseling
      - Crisis intervention
      - Mental health referrals
      
      Visit [Counseling Services](https://www.dallascollege.edu/resources/counseling/pages/default.aspx) for support based on your campus location.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="How do I get accommodations for my disability?">
      Contact Accessibility Services to get the accommodations you need:
      1. Visit [Accessibility Services](https://www.dallascollege.edu/resources/accessibility-services/pages/default.aspx)
      2. Follow the process for getting accommodations
    </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>
</div>
